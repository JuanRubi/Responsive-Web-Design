Hi! I'm Juan.

Life can be overwhelming between work, family, and bills. Not to mention any unexpected events. It can be hard to keep track of everything.

Things were constantly appearing, new projects for college, needing to work more hours to cover tution, and making sure my family had a roof over their head.

How I turned things around

I had to differentiate the important from the unimportant things first. So, I created a schedule to see where my time was being spent and got rid of anything I considered unimportant. Giving me back control of my day to day life.

Now I live the simple life

Now I am less stressed and have a solid understanding of what needs to get done. I encourage others to look inward and asks yourselves, What is important to me? Then get rid of everything that isn't.
